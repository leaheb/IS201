THE TAYLOR SWIFT RESUME
 
Now that you've read about my professional accomplishments, it's time for me to educate you on one of my biggest passions: Taylor Swift. Buckle in!
 

CREDENTIALS

I've loved Taylor Swift since I was nine years old. I heard "Love Story" in the backseat of a friend's van on the way home from school, and the rest was history. She's been with me through family dance parties, my first day of high school, a lot of crushes, my first love, and my first heartbreak. The more she creates, the more I love her. So, as you read, know that you're learning from a qualified Taylor Swift expert of almost fifteen years!

ERAS

Taylor Swift's music spans decades, genres, and stages of life. From beginning to end, these are each of those albums.

  1. Taylor Swift
    • Released in 2006
    • Lead single, "Tim McGraw", certified as platinum
    • Finding and losing young love as the girl next door
  2. Fearless
    • Released in 2008
    • Won Album of the Year, making Swift the youngest artisit to ever win that award
    • Young love, pining, and hope for the future
  3. Speak Now
    • Released in 2010
    • Completey self-written by Swift alone
    • Love as something magical, enchanting, and sometimes tragic
  4. Red
    • Released in 2012
    • Lead single, "We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together", was Swift's first #1 on the Billboard Hot 100
    • Stages of heartbreak and finding love again afterwards
  5. 1989
    • Released in 2014
    • Sold over a million copies in its first week, won "Album of the Year", making Swift the first woman to ever win that award twice
    • Finding joy in the adventures of singlehood
  6. reputation
    • Released in 2017
    • Revenge on the world and refuge in newfound romance
  7. Lover
    • Released in 2019
    • The first album owned 100% by Swift
    • Love in its many forms: romantic, maternal, and platonic
  8. folklore
    • Released in 2020
    • Won "Album of the Year", making Swift the first woman to win that award three times
    • Lives, loves and dreams of other characters besides Swift
  9. evermore
    • Released in 2020
    • Companion album to "folklore"
    • Continued stories of characters other than Swift
  10. Midnights
    • Released in 2022
    • First album to have ten songs be 1-10 on Billboard Hot 100
    • Sleepless nights throughout Swift's life
